Google Font Family

Apr 11, 2021 by Dafont 1 Comment

On September 1, 2015, Google made its most radical logo alteration ever by switching out their old serif font for Product Sans, replacing Catull with it.

Google has updated their logo design with multicolored, vibrant playfulness and the iconic flipped “e” still present from their previous mark. They have also revised their icon, favicon and logo – the small “g” has been replaced with a four-colored G placed within Product Sans.
